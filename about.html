About StudyScapes

StudyScapes is written entirely HTML and CSS, with just a bit of JavaScript It was created for BrickHacks 2022. It was inspired by imissmycafe, imissmylibrary, and imissmybar. All images are from StorySet, except for the map which was created with an outline from Canva. All icons are from Flaticon. All sound effects are from Zapslat and Mixkit. Thank you for visiting the site!
